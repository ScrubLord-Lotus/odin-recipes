Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Description

A googy delightful meal that would be perfect for breakfast lunch or dinner.

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

    • Optional

    • Tomatoe

Steps

  1. Heat hot plate
  2. Butter both sides of bread
  3. Place one slice onto heated surface
  4. Place cheese on bread
  5. Place other piece of bread on top
  6. Cook for three mintues
  7. Flip and cook for another three mintues
